Bring woodland magic to your community with Squonk & Friends, a family-friendly, hour-long circus performance. Featuring silks, lyra, rope, and partner acrobatics, the show follows Squonk—the shy Pennsylvania cryptid who dissolves into tears when seen.

As woodland companions try to lift Squonk’s spirits, audiences experience a moving story of self-acceptance and grief. Now booking for schools, libraries, nonprofits, and youth organizations.
